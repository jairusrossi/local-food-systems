Local Food Resources in Eastern Kentucky

This map shows assets related to the food system in eastern Kentucky. Mapped locations represent areas of food production, exchange, processing, and technical expertise.

How to Use this Map:

Select the resource type of interest in the dropdown box in the top-right corner. New points will appear based on the category selected.

The legend in the bottom right corner will also change based on this category. If your mouse arrow hovers over a point on the map, information about that business will appear.

Information:

  • Data gathered from KADIS, the USDA SNAP program and 2012 Ag Census, the KDA KCARD.
    • Map authored by Jairus Rossi
